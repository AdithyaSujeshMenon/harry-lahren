Style Guide

Mastering the Art of Layering for Autumn

As the leaves begin to turn and the air grows crisp, the sartorial landscape shifts. Autumn is arguably the most exciting season for menswear, offering a rich palette of textures, fabrics, and silhouettes to play with. The key to navigating this transition with elegance lies in one fundamental skill: layering.

Layering is not merely a practical necessity for fluctuating temperatures; it is an opportunity for expression. It allows for the juxtaposition of materials—pairing the roughness of tweed with the softness of cashmere, or the structure of denim with the fluidity of silk.

"True style is not about standing out, but being remembered."

Start with a solid foundation. A high-quality cotton shirt or a fine merino wool roll-neck serves as the perfect base. From there, introduce a mid-layer such as a cashmere cardigan or a tailored waistcoat. This adds depth and visual interest without compromising on comfort.

The final layer—the outerwear—is your statement piece. Whether it's a classic trench coat for rainy London afternoons or a structured wool overcoat for formal occasions, ensure it complements the layers beneath rather than concealing them.

The Autumn Palette

Autumn's natural color palette offers endless inspiration. Think rich burgundies, deep forest greens, warm caramels, and sophisticated charcoals. These hues not only complement the season but also work harmoniously together, making it easier to create cohesive outfits. At Harry Lahren, we've carefully curated our autumn collection to reflect these timeless tones, ensuring each piece can be mixed and matched effortlessly.

Don't shy away from texture either. The interplay between smooth and rough, matte and lustrous, adds visual depth to your ensemble. A brushed cotton shirt paired with a silk pocket square, or corduroy trousers with a smooth leather belt—these subtle contrasts elevate your look from ordinary to exceptional.

Fabric Matters

The choice of fabric is paramount in autumn dressing. Merino wool offers warmth without bulk, making it ideal for base layers. Cashmere provides unparalleled softness and insulation for mid-layers. For outerwear, look to heavyweight wools, waxed cotton, or technical fabrics that offer both protection and breathability.

Each fabric in our collection is selected not just for its aesthetic appeal, but for its performance. We work exclusively with mills that have been perfecting their craft for generations, ensuring that every garment you invest in will serve you well for years to come.

Practical Styling Tips

Start your day with a versatile base that can adapt to changing temperatures. A crisp white shirt or neutral roll-neck provides the perfect foundation. Keep a lightweight cardigan or blazer at the office for cooler mornings, and invest in a quality overcoat that can transition from business meetings to evening events.

Remember, successful layering is about balance. If your outer layers are voluminous, keep your base layers fitted. Conversely, if you're wearing a slim-cut coat, you can afford slightly more relaxed layers underneath. The goal is to create a silhouette that looks intentional, not accidental.

At Harry Lahren, our Autumn collection is designed with this interplay in mind. Each piece is crafted to work in harmony with the next, creating a cohesive wardrobe that is as functional as it is stylish. We invite you to visit our Savile Row flagship or explore the collection online to discover how these principles can transform your autumn wardrobe.
