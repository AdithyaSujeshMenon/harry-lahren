Frequently Asked Questions

We offer Standard Shipping (3-5 business days) and Express Shipping (1-2 business days). International shipping is also available to over 50 countries.

Returns are accepted within 30 days of purchase. Simply visit our Returns Portal to generate a prepaid shipping label. Items must be unworn and in original packaging.

Yes, complimentary alterations are available for suits and trousers purchased in-store. For online orders, we recommend visiting a local tailor for the perfect fit.

Our garments are crafted in Italy, Portugal, and the United Kingdom, working with partners who share our commitment to quality and ethical labor practices.

Please refer to our comprehensive Size Guide which includes detailed measurements for all garment types. We recommend measuring a well-fitting garment you already own and comparing it to our size charts. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more relaxed fit.

Yes, our Savile Row flagship offers full bespoke tailoring services. The process begins with a personal consultation, followed by multiple fittings to ensure a perfect fit. Bespoke suits typically take 8-12 weeks to complete. Please contact our Savile Row location to schedule a consultation.

Care instructions are specific to each garment and can be found on the care label. Generally, we recommend dry cleaning for suits and tailored pieces, while knitwear should be hand-washed in cold water and laid flat to dry. For specific care advice, please consult our detailed Care Guide or contact our customer service team.

Yes, once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and viewing your order history. If you checked out as a guest, use the tracking link provided in your shipping confirmation email.

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination but typically range from 5-10 business days. Please note that customs duties and import taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient. These charges are not included in your order total.

Gift cards are delivered instantly via email and can be used both online and in-store. They're valid for 12 months from the date of purchase and can be applied to any Harry Lahren product. Gift cards are non-refundable but can be combined with other payment methods if the purchase exceeds the card value.

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After this window, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed. If you need to make changes, please contact our customer service team immediately at customercare@harrylahren.com or call +44 20 7123 4567.

We're committed to sustainable practices throughout our supply chain. This includes sourcing from certified sustainable mills, implementing zero-waste cutting techniques, offering a garment repair service to extend product life, and using recyclable packaging materials. We also partner with factories that maintain fair labor practices and safe working conditions.
